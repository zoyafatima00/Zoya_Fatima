Property Asia - Explore and Purchase Real Estate

The Property Asia app was developed to provide users with a convenient platform for discovering and purchasing properties.

I played a significant role in the development of this project, focusing on creating a user-friendly frontend that allows seamless navigation and property browsing.

My contributions included integrating APIs to ensure smooth data retrieval and synchronization, as well as implementing functionality to manage user permissions effectively.

I also worked on integrating Google Maps, enabling users to view property locations and access directions with ease.

Our team aimed to deliver a comprehensive solution for real estate enthusiasts, continuously enhancing the app to meet user expectations.

Technologies:
  • Flutter: Developed the app's cross-platform interface, providing a consistent experience on Android and iOS devices.
  • Dart: Utilized for building the app's architecture and handling business logic.
  • Google Maps API: Integrated to provide location-based services and map functionalities.
  • RESTful APIs: Connected the frontend with backend services to enable dynamic data flow.
  • Git: Used for version control, ensuring a smooth development process.
  • GitHub: Facilitated collaborative development and code management.
  • Android Studio Code: Served as the primary development environment for coding and debugging.
